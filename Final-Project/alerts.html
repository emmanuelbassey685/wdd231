Wind Safety Instructions

  • Stay indoors away from windows.

  • Watch out for falling trees and power lines.

Flood Safety Instructions

  • Never walk or drive through flooded roads.
  • Move to higher ground if local waters rise.
  • Keep emergency kits easily reachable.
